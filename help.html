Data Privacy Act

Republic Act No. 10173 - Data Privacy Act of 2012

This is the primary data privacy law in the Philippines. It aims to protect the fundamental human right to privacy of information while ensuring free flow of information.

Key Provisions:

  • Personal information must be processed lawfully and fairly
  • Data subjects have rights to access, rectify, and erase their personal data
  • Organizations must implement reasonable and appropriate security measures
  • Unauthorized processing of personal information is prohibited
  • Data breaches must be reported to the National Privacy Commission

All feedback submitted through this system is protected under RA 10173 and is handled with utmost confidentiality.
